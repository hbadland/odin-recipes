Chicken Pasta

Description

This is a tasty high protein chicken pasta

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Herbs

Instructions

  1. Begin by boiling the pasta
  2. Dice the chicken and season it
  3. Fry the chicken in the pan
  4. Add the tomato sauce to the chicken
  5. Drain the pasta once cooked
  6. Mix all together
