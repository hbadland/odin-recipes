Lasagna

Description

In this recipe, to keep things simple, we chose to layer our lasagna as pasta on bottom, then meat mixture and cheese then back to the pasta and repeating. Finally, we finish with the sauce and a few slices of fresh mozzarella cheese. But you can mix things up if you want. Just don't make the sauce layer too thick. The lasagna might not hold together well without the structure of the noodles.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  1. Start by making the lasagna
  2. Then cook it
  3. Let it cool a bit
  4. Now eat it
